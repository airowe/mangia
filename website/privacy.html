Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Overview

Mangia ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Mangia, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and name to identify you and provide personalized services.

Recipe Data

We store the recipes you import or create, including ingredients, instructions, and any notes you add. This data is synced to our servers to enable access across your devices.

Pantry & Grocery Data

Information about items in your pantry and grocery lists is stored to provide features like "What Can I Make?" and smart grocery list generation.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including which features are used and general app performance metrics. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To personalize your experience and provide recipe recommendations
  • To improve our app based on usage patterns
  • To send you important updates about the service (you can opt out)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for our database infrastructure, which provides enterprise-grade security including:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Regular security audits
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Clerk - For authentication and user management
  • Supabase - For database and storage
  • Vercel - For API hosting

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@mangia.app.

Children's Privacy

Mangia is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@mangia.app
  • Support: mangia.app/support
