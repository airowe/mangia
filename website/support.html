FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a recipe?

To import a recipe:

  1. Tap the + button on the home screen
  2. Select "Import from URL"
  3. Paste the recipe URL (from TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or any food blog)
  4. Tap "Import" and our AI will extract the recipe automatically

You can also use the iOS Share Sheet to import directly from your browser or social media apps.

What sources can I import recipes from?

Mangia can import recipes from virtually any source:

  • TikTok videos
  • YouTube videos
  • Instagram posts and reels
  • Food blogs (AllRecipes, Serious Eats, Bon Appetit, etc.)
  • Pinterest pins
  • Any webpage with recipe content

How does pantry tracking work?

Your pantry is your inventory of ingredients at home. You can:

  • Manually add items with quantities and expiration dates
  • Scan grocery receipts (Premium) to bulk-add items
  • Get alerts when items are expiring soon
  • See which recipes you can make with what you have

When you generate a grocery list, Mangia automatically deducts items you already have in your pantry.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free tier includes:

  • Up to 10 saved recipes
  • Basic pantry tracking
  • Grocery list generation
  • Cooking mode

Premium ($4.99/month) adds:

  • Unlimited recipes
  • AI receipt scanning
  • Voice control in cooking mode
  • Weekly meal planning
  • Priority support

How do I cancel my subscription?

Since subscriptions are managed through Apple, you'll need to cancel via your iPhone:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find Mangia and tap "Cancel Subscription"

You'll continue to have access to Premium features until the end of your current billing period.

How do I use cooking mode?

Cooking mode provides a hands-free experience while cooking:

  1. Open any recipe
  2. Tap "Start Cooking"
  3. Swipe left/right to navigate between steps
  4. Say "Hey Siri, next step" for voice control (Premium)
  5. Tap the timer icon to set step-specific timers

The screen stays awake and text is extra large for easy reading.

Can I export my recipes?

Yes! You can export your recipes at any time:

  1. Go to Settings > Account
  2. Tap "Export My Data"
  3. Choose your format (JSON or PDF)
  4. Your data will be emailed to you

Is my data backed up?

Yes, your data is automatically synced to our secure cloud servers. This means:

  • Your recipes are safe even if you lose your phone
  • You can access your recipes on a new device by signing in
  • Changes sync automatically across devices
